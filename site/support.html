Support

How can we help?

SwarSaathi is building Indian music learning tools, beginning with SwarPractice, a free real-time swara tuner for singers and learners.

SwarPractice Checks

  • Allow microphone access when prompted.
  • Set "Your Pitch" to your madhya Sa.
  • Use headphones when tanpura or metronome is enabled.
  • Switch between Riyaz and Accompaniment from the mode tabs.
  • Unavailable tanpura pitches are disabled when accompaniment needs a matching loop.
  • Keep "Speaker-test octave correction" off for normal singing.

App Store Review Notes

SwarPractice needs microphone access for live pitch detection. Analysis runs on your device and is not uploaded. Optional session recordings stay on your device until you share or delete them. Tanpura accompaniment may stream from SwarSaathi audio storage; that audio is not a microphone recording.
