About SwarSaathi

Technology in service of attentive practice.

SwarSaathi makes Indian music practice clearer while keeping listening, teachers, and tradition at the heart of learning.

Our starting point

One difficult practice moment, made easier.

A learner sings a note and wonders: Which swara am I on? Am I centred? Am I holding it steadily? SwarPractice begins there, with immediate Sa-relative feedback and an interface designed to stay quiet while the learner listens.

Over time, SwarSaathi will connect pitch, swara, accompaniment, and guided learning in one musical journey.

Our approach

Designed for thoughtful riyaz.

Listen first

Visual feedback supports the ear instead of competing with it.

Musically careful

Notes, tuning, and accompaniment should feel trustworthy to the learner.

Calm and clear

A focused interface leaves more attention for the sound.

Private practice

Live microphone analysis stays on the learner’s device.
