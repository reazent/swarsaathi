Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

SwarSaathi builds Indian music learning tools. The first app, SwarPractice, helps learners practice swaras, saptak, and pitch stability.

Microphone

SwarPractice uses your microphone only to detect pitch in real time. Audio is processed on your device for the current practice session. SwarPractice v1 does not upload or store microphone audio.

Practice Data

Session results such as pitch accuracy, stability, and cents deviation are shown to you in the app. In the current free v1, this data is not synced to an account.

Analytics And Diagnostics

We may use privacy-conscious analytics and crash reporting to understand app reliability and improve the learning experience. If enabled, this may include device type, app version, crash logs, and general usage events. We do not sell personal data.

Future Song Tools

Future SwarSaathi song pitch or notation tools may analyze official recordings or user-provided references. Any new data practices will be reflected in this policy before those features are released publicly.

Children

SwarSaathi is a general music education tool. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the SwarSaathi team at privacy@swarsaathi.com.
