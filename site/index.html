Sing → see → improve

A clear feedback loop for daily riyaz.

No account or setup maze. Choose your Sa, allow the microphone when you are ready, and begin with one note.

  1. 01

    Set Your Sa

    Choose the tonic that matches your voice or instrument. This keeps swara names grounded in your practice pitch.

  2. 02

    Sing and see

    Watch the detected swara, register, accuracy, and stability while you hold or move between notes.

  3. 03

    Review and repeat

    Use the session summary—and optional on-device recordings—to hear less guesswork and make the next repetition intentional.
