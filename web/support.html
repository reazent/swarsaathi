SwarPractice Support

SwarPractice is a free real-time swara tuner from SwarSaathi.

Common Checks

  • Allow microphone access when prompted.
  • Set “Your Pitch” to your madhya Sa.
  • Use headphones when tanpura or metronome is enabled.
  • Switch between Riyaz and Accompaniment from the mode tabs.
  • Unavailable tanpura pitches are disabled when accompaniment needs a matching loop.
  • Keep “Speaker-test octave correction” off for normal singing.

Contact

Contact support@swarsaathi.com.

App Store Review Notes

The app needs microphone access only for live pitch detection. Audio is processed on device and is not uploaded in SwarPractice v1. Tanpura recordings are streamed from SwarSaathi audio storage and are not microphone recordings.
