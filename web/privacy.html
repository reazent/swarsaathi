Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

SwarPractice by SwarSaathi helps Indian music learners practice swara, saptak, and pitch stability.

Microphone

SwarPractice uses your microphone to detect pitch in real time. Live analysis runs on your device and is not uploaded to SwarSaathi servers.

If you turn on Record this session, microphone audio is saved locally on your device until you share or delete it. Sharing uses your device’s share sheet or download flow. SwarSaathi does not upload recordings for you.

Practice Data

Session results such as pitch accuracy, stability, and cents deviation are shown to you in the app. This data is not synced to an account in the current free release.

Analytics And Diagnostics

We may use privacy-conscious analytics and crash reporting to understand app reliability and improve the learning experience. If enabled, this may include device type, app version, crash logs, and general usage events. We do not sell personal data.

Children

SwarPractice is a general music education tool. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy as SwarSaathi adds accounts, cloud sync, or additional learning tools.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact privacy@swarsaathi.com.
