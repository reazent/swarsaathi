## Full description

SwarSaathi is an Indian music learning app. The first included tool, SwarPractice, is a free real-time swara tuner for Indian music learners.

Set your Sa, sing, and instantly see the swara, saptak, pitch, and tuning feedback. SwarPractice supports Mandra, Madhya, and Taar saptak notation, including dot markers above or below the swara. Practice freely or use short timed drills to build sur stability.

Current features:

- Select your Sa from common Indian vocal pitch ranges
- Detect swara and saptak in real time
- Standard komal, shuddha, tivra, mandra, and taar notation
- Timed practice sessions: 5s, 10s, 20s, 30s
- Free practice mode
- Accuracy and stability summary after practice
- Pitch bias and cents spread feedback
- Optional tanpura and metronome practice support
- On-device microphone processing

SwarSaathi is designed for learners of Indian classical, semi-classical, film, and devotional music who want a simple way to understand notes, practice sur, and improve steadily.

## Review notes

SwarPractice uses the microphone for real-time pitch detection. Audio is processed on device and is not uploaded in v1. To test, select Sa = C3, tap Start listening, allow microphone access, and sing a sustained note.
